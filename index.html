Biography

When I was eight years old, my mom decided that I should pick up either the violin or the piano. I didn't know what either of those words meant, so I went with the one I heard first. Thanks, mom!

After years of classical training, I grew fond of the violin, but along the way I fell in love with a dream much greater: learning every instrument I could. Since then I've picked up the violin, viola, cello, double bass, trombone, and piano, and it's been the greatest gift of my musical career—especially taking the plunge and switching over to viola.

Somehow one thing led to another, and I started traveling the world performing in places like Costa Rica's National Theater and Taiwan's Presidential Office Building. Back at home in Los Angeles, I've played in everything from short films and Broadway musicals to string quartets and symphony orchestras.

But at the end of the day, I still feel like that same eight-year-old kid at heart: noodling around on whatever instruments I can find, chasing new sounds, and trying to use music to navigate this crazy ride we call life. Thanks for joining me!

Frequently Asked Questions

Music

What's a viola (and why should I care)?

Great question! Imagine if the violin and the cello had a (way cooler) middle sibling. Due to the viola's unique construction, it resonates differently than other string instruments, resulting in a sound that packs the elegance of a violin and the warmth of a cello, all in one package.

Compared to many other instruments, there aren't a lot of us viola players out there, so booking one is a great way to make your wedding or event stand out! ;)

Can you learn a song for my event?

Yes! If you don't see what you're looking for in my repertoire, I would be happy to learn any and all songs you'd like. A small additional fee may apply.

Can you help me choose songs?

Yes! If you'd like some help topping off your setlist, or if you just have a general vibe in mind, I'd be happy to work with you to fill in the gaps based on my recommendations.

Do you use backing tracks?

Yes! I use backing tracks to add more dimension to the music. Depending on the song and on your event needs, this will either be the full instrumental track from the original song, or a stripped piano/acoustic track.

Can you play with a group?

I can play in a duet, trio, or quartet with other strings and/or a piano by request. Please be advised that some pieces in my repertoire may not be available with groups.

Can you play a different instrument?

I can play violin, electric 5-string violin, or cello by request. Please be advised that some pieces in my repertoire may not be available on these instruments.

Logistics

How much do you charge?

I only name starting rates on this website because your exact quote may exceed this depending on several factors, primarily the length of the performance, the venue location, and the amount of custom song requests outside of my repertoire.

To get a personalized quote, please don't hesitate to reach out!

When/how do you collect payments?

Once we've agreed on a rate, a 25% deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remainder of your quote will be due after the performance.

I typically collect payments through Venmo or Zelle. However, if neither of these platforms work for you, I am happy to figure out an alternative!

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Your 25% deposit will be forfeited in the event of a cancellation.

I am happy to reschedule bookings at no cost, provided the new date and time still works for me.

How far in advance do I need to book?

I will gladly consider all bookings, regardless of how near or far into the future the occasion is!

That being said, I reserve the right to decline bookings made less than 30 days in advance on the grounds of insufficient preparation time.

Are you willing to travel?

Yes! I'm based in Southern California, but I'm happy to go wherever on the planet your event takes us. An additional fee may apply for anything past an hour of travel from Glendora, CA.

How long can you play for?

I don't have a strict minimum or maximum booking length!

That being said, for bookings of 2 hours or longer, I reserve the right to take a short 5-10 minute break after each hour of playing, during which I'll play canned music through my sound setup.

Contact

You can reach me at hello@jeffviolist.com for all bookings and inquiries. I look forward to working with you!
